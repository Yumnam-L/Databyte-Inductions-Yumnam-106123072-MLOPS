Paddle movement Left: Left Arrow key.  Right: Right Arrow key

The golden bricks are "Power-up" Surprise bricks and they randomly assign you a power after you destroy them. This randomness brings exitement to the game.
You can also utilise the Power-up buttons they last for 5s and they will cost you 3 points from your score.
Increase volume to hear the full sound effect. You win the game if you are able to destroy all the bricks before they reach the floor.
